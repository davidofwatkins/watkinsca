Greetings, and welcome to my page! If you didn't already know, I'm the college student of the family. I attended Sage Hill School in Newport Coast, CA before recently attending Champlain College in Burlington, Vermont. I'm currently studying Computer Information Technology (CIT), although I'm planning on specializing more specifically in web development in the future. In fact, this website is an example of my work. I created everything you see here from scratch using straight HTML and CSS as a personal project.

 

As you can probably tell, I am very interested in technology—everything from building and restoring computers to exploring the secrets of Android; from keeping up to date with the latest and greatest software to wandering the depths of Lifehacker, Gizmodo, and Google News; from praising Google to bashing Apple. (That last part's only half true.) Of course, I have other interests as well, such as theatre, film, photography, and music.

 

But why am I telling you this? Well, mostly because this page needs filler content, but also because I figured I should have some kind of personal introduction on the Watkins website. If you'd like to further connect with me, be sure to check out some of the links to the left.

 

Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you have time to explore some other parts of the site—like our Christmas card collection, or our family tree!

 

Cheers for now,
David O.
